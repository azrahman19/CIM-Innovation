Session Highlights

Key Takeaways

Copilot Chat is available to everyone at Maersk and can be used for prompt-based productivity immediately, even without a Premium license.

Meeting recap workaround: Download your Teams transcript, then paste it into Copilot Chat to generate a structured recap with action items.

Premium Copilot licenses are limited to approximately 3,000 across Maersk. Features like automated meeting summaries require this licence, but core Copilot Chat is universally accessible.

Two prompts published on AI Hub: The Asana Project Logo prompt and the Volume Tracker prompt are live and tagged for CIM. Try them and contribute your own.

Innovation Champion recognition will be awarded by year-end based on community consensus and impact. Start contributing ideas now.
